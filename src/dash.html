Welcome Recruiter!

Do you want to verify whether you candidate owns the certificate. Request them for their certificate_id and view it right here!


About

Certificate verification is as easy as entering a certificate ID and a digital signature to validate and prove ownership of the certification.

Advantages:
- The certificates are trustlessly verifiable.
- You would not need to contact the issuer directly as the data is already on the blockchain.

How does it work?

During the issuance, the student gets a program_id. This program_id can be used to scan the blockchain by Resume Explorer and look into its data. Fields such as isCertified can only be set by the decentralized program (which is also public), These data can be used to verify the person owns the degree.
